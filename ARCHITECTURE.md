## Key Design Principles

1. **Minimal Code**: Only 159 new lines total
2. **Zero Dependencies**: Native browser APIs only
3. **Event-Driven**: No polling, no background tasks
4. **Type-Safe**: Full TypeScript coverage
5. **Performance-First**: Fixed presets, optimized chains
6. **Graceful Degradation**: Works without MIDI/audio
7. **Clean Architecture**: Hooks are isolated, testable
